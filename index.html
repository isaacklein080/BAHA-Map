About This Data

Source: Berkeley Architectural Heritage Association (BAHA)

What's Included: This map displays officially designated historic landmarks. However, it's important to recognize what's not shown:

  • Undesignated sites significant to Indigenous Ohlone communities
  • Places important to communities of color that lack official recognition
  • Recent cultural sites not yet considered "historic"

Critical Note: Historic preservation often reflects the perspectives and values of those in power. This map represents one narrative of Berkeley's cultural significance.
